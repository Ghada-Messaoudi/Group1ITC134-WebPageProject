Cygwin Installation Steps

Let's assume that your USB flash drive is on E:

  1. In your FileExplorer, create a folder at E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable\packages
  2. Download the Cygwin installer executable file (*.exe filename). In a web browser, goto https://www.cygwin.com/install.html . Click on setup-x86_64.exe link to download into your Downloads folder.
  3. In your FileExplorer, copy from your Downloads folder the “setup-x86_64.exe” file to your E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable directory.
  4. Install Cygwin by clicking upon the “setup-x86_64.exe” file.
  5. In new window “User Account Control” click “Yes”
  6. Click “Next>” on first dialog install window.
  7. On next window, select “Install from Internet”. Then click “Next>”.
  8. In next window, browse or type “E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable” to be root directory.
  9. Then select “All Users (RECOMMENDED)” for install. Then click “Next>”.
  10. On next window, browse or type “E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable\packages” for Local Package Directory. Then click “Next>”.
  11. In next window, select “Direct Connection” for Select Your Internet Connection. Then click “Next”.
  12. The next window asks for Choose A Download Site. In your web browser, goto https://cygwin.com/mirrors.html . From here, determine the best site to download nearest to your location. For the Seattle Central College (SCC) students, choose Washington: http://mirrors.metapeer.com . Then click “Next>”.
  13. Next page will be downloading status page. When done, the window will close and a new window will open called “Select Packages”.
  14. In “Select Packages” window, click “Next>”.
  15. In next window, ensure/select “Select required packages (RECOMMENDED)”. Then click “Next>”.
  16. Next page will be ANOTHER downloading status page. THIS WILL TAKE ABOUT FIVE (5) TO TEN (10)MINUTES OF TIME WAITING. When done, the window will close and a new window will open called “Create Icons” and “Installation Status”. Make sure NO boxes are selected/checked. Then click “Finish”. This means on the bare-bones default Cygwin packages have been installed.
  17. Now that the Cygwin install is done, you need to create the command-line Terminal Window short-cut on USB Stick. So, in your FileExplorer, in folder “E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable”, right-click and select “New >” then select “Shortcut”. In “Create Shortcut” window, type “E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable\bin\mintty.exe -i /Cygwin-Terminal.ico -” . Then click on “Next “. In next window, type in “Cygwin_Linux_Terminal”

. . . . . . NOW, you can finally double-click on “Cygwin_Linux_Terminal” icon to launch a Cygwin-Linux Command Line Terminal window with “bash” linux-shell. Now, keep this Terminal-Window open while we setup gVim text-editor and TkDiff code-debugger tool.

gVim Installation Steps

  1. In a web browser, goto http://www.vim.org/download.html . Click on “self-installing executable” link in order to download gVim installing executable file.
  2. In your FileExplorer, copy from your Downloads folder the “gvim[VERSION_NUMBER].exe” file to your E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable directory. For this cheatsheet, we will call this file “gvim80-586.exe”.
  3. Install gVim by clicking upon the “gvim80-586.exe” file.
  4. In new window “User Account Control” click “Yes”
  5. In next window, click “Yes”.
  6. In new window, click “I Agree” .
  7. In next window, click “Next >”
  8. In new window, browse or type “E:\PortableApps\CygwinPortable\Vim”. Click on “Install” button.
  9. In “Completed” window, click “Close” button.
  10. In next window, click “No” button.
